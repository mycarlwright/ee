Civil & Structural Engineering

Civil Engineer / Infrastructure

Civil Engineer with at least two years post qual experience gained in the UK. Join a Norfolk based multi-disciplinary team, bringing consultants, architects, and civil & structural engineers together to deliver award-winning projects.

The role

An experienced Civil Engineer or Design Engineer providing detailed design, site and coordination support, you'll be involved in drainage and pavement designs on a wide variety of civil and infrastructure projects.

This is not just a design role you'll enjoy project coordination and work closely with PMs, Surveyors and contractor teams to deliver schemes at all stages.

It is a hands-on role where you will also contribute to project management plans, prepare reports and visit sites to report on technical feasibility and design compliance.

What's on offer

Join a team where your judgement is trusted, your experience is valued and you'll be encouraged to help mentor others. A genuinely varied project portfolio from education, retail, commercial, medical and research facilities through to award-winning residential, decarbonisation and civil engineering projects. Salary £32-35k + car + benefits to reflect your breadth of project experience.

Support to gain further professional status with the ICE.

About you

  • Ideally 3 years of UK civil design experience gained in various materials
  • A strong eye for quality and detail and the standards knowledge to back it.
  • Based locally in Norfolk and a willingness to work across the region
  • Basic temporary works knowledge would be an advantage
